Designed and built a framework that can interpret an image, identify contextually unfit objects within it and remove these objects to enhance the image. Our model used CNN and Mask RCNN for understanding the scene and segmenting various objects within the image. Context Model and Latent Tree were used to identify out of context objects and exemplar-based inpainting was used to eliminate these objects. A key application of the model is to detect unusual activities such as fires and accidents by processing live camera feed in real-time while it can also use the latter part of the model to enhance an image by eliminating background noise.
